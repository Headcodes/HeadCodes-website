Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

What will I learn in this course?

Throughout this course, you will learn essential front-end development skills such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, version control with Git, and using popular libraries and frameworks like Bootstrap and jQuery.

Who is this course for?

This course is designed for beginners who want to learn front-end web development from scratch. It's also suitable for individuals with some basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript who want to enhance their skills

Do I need any prior knowledge or experience?

No prior knowledge or experience is required to enroll in this course. However, having a basic understanding of how to use a computer and navigate the internet will be beneficial.

How do I enroll in the course?

To enroll in the course, simply visit our website and follow the instructions for registration. After registration, our team will contact you with further details.

How long does the course take to complete?

The duration of the course may vary depending on your learning pace and the amount of time you dedicate to studying each week. On average, students complete the course within 3 to 6 months.

Is there any support available during the course?

Yes, you will have access to our dedicated support team who will assist you with any questions or technical issues you may encounter during the course. Additionally, you can interact with fellow students through our online community forums.
